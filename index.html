Types of Bets

INDIBET supports various types of bets, including:

  • Single Bets: A bet on a single outcome in one event.
  • Accumulator Bets: Multiple bets combined into one, where all selections must win for the bet to be successful.
  • System Bets: A series of bets that cover multiple combinations within a selection, providing a safety net if some selections lose.
